University of La Verne Major Events Calendar

The University Major Events Calendar is intended to provide an overview of University wide events and key dates.   It should not be considered an exhaustive list.   For more information on events and key dates please visit:

  • Academic Calendars go to https://laverne.edu/academics/calendars/ 
  • Student Events go to laverne.campuslabs.com/engage (log-in required)
  • Leopard Athletic Events go to https://www.leopardathletics.com/landing/index
    • Go list of events taking place on ULV Stadium go to Stadium Schedule
  • Dining Services days of operation go to https://laverne.cafebonappetit.com/#board-calendar

To schedule an event on campus go to https://laverne.edu/acc/events/.  To request an event or key date be added to University Major Events Calendar contact vashcroft@laverne.edu.
